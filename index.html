about us

The Jazz Café is located in the vibrant and lively Temple Bar area of Dublin. In 2015 the award-winning jazz restaurant celebrated 15 years of presenting sensational jazz entertainment paired with gourmet cuisine.

Our intimate, elegant atmosphere is the perfect setting to enjoy an evening of world-class entertainment enhanced by our innovative menu by our award-winning chef Jean le Moritte and our personal, attentive service.

To book your table for the whole evening - just a rolling stone's throw from the stage! From our fresh delicious food, to our extensive wine and drinks selection - we aim to bring you the best in fine dining and live Jazz - making your visit a night to remember.

Come along and soak up the atmosphere, listen to fabulous music, enjoy great food, wine and cocktails at The Jazz Café.

Jean le Moritte

Owner and Head Chef
